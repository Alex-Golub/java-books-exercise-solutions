Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portra of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére was painted in 1806 by the french Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Diminique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviére family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musée du Louvre, Paris
